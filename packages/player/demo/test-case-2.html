Test Case 2

  • Go to an album page
  • Select a track to play
  • Play the track
  • After 20 seconds scrub to the last 20 seconds of the song
  • Finish the track

Expectations

  • 1 record logged
  • PLAYBACK_STOP action logged at assetPosition = 20 sec
  • PLAYBACK_START action logged at track length - 20 sec
  • startAssetPosition = 0
  • endAssetPosition = track length
  • actualProductId = trackId played
  • sourceType = “ALBUM”
  • sourceId = albumId of played track
